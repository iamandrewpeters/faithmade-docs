# Setting Up Connect Flows

Connect Flows are digital next-step cards that make it easy for people to take action — volunteer, request prayer, join a group, say "I'm new here." Each one is a clear path forward.

## Creating a Connect Flow

1. Go to **Connect Flows > Add New**
2. Give it a clear title ("I'm New Here" or "Request Prayer")
3. Add a short description of what happens when they click
4. Upload an image that fits the vibe
5. Set your action link (where does this send them?)
6. Hit **Publish**

Connect Flows appear on your site through the Page Builder — drop a Connect section onto any page and your flows show up as clean, clickable cards.

## Ideas That Just Work

Most churches start with:

- I'm New Here
- Join a Group
- Request Prayer
- Serve With Us
- I Said Yes (salvation or baptism response)

:::tip
Don't overdo it. 4–6 flows is the sweet spot. Too many choices and people pick nothing.
:::
