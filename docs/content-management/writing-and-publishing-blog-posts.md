# Writing & Publishing Blog Posts

Your Faithmade site comes with a built-in blog. Weekly updates, devotionals, event recaps, staff spotlights — your content, your way. No third-party plugins needed.

## Creating a Post

1. Go to **Blog > Add New**
2. Add your title
3. Write your content using the block editor (paragraph, heading, image, list, and quote blocks are all there)
4. Upload a featured image (this shows in your blog feed, so make it count)
5. Assign categories and tags to keep things organized
6. Hit **Publish** when you're ready

Not ready to go live? Save it as a **Draft** and come back to it. You can also schedule posts to publish on a future date — perfect for that "written at midnight, publish at 9am" workflow.

## Tips for Your Church Blog

- **Keep posts scannable.** Short paragraphs, bold key points, use headings. Your readers are on their phones between meetings.
- **Be consistent.** One post a week beats five posts in January and then silence until Easter.
- **Write like you talk.** If you wouldn't say it from the stage, don't write it on the blog.

Edit or trash posts anytime from the blog list view.
