# Adding a New Event

Adding events to your Faithmade site takes a few minutes — and they look great on your events page without any extra fuss.

## Creating Your Event

1. Click **Events** in the left sidebar, then **Add New**
2. Give it a clear title ("Men's Breakfast" works — "Event #47" does not)
3. Use the block editor to add your description — think about the first-timer: what do they need to know to feel comfortable showing up?
4. Add an **Event Subtitle** for a short tagline like "A morning of food and fellowship"

## Setting the Schedule

Fill in your **Start Date/Time** and **End Date/Time**. Toggle **All Day** for retreats or conferences. The **After Service** toggle is a handy flag for post-service events.

:::tip
Double-check your AM/PM. Nothing like advertising your pancake breakfast at 7:00 PM.
:::

## The Finishing Touches

Upload a **Featured Image**, assign **Event Types** and **Departments** for filtering, and flip the **Featured** toggle if you want it front and center on your events page.

Hit **Publish** and your new event is live.
