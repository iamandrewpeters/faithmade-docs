# Event List & Management

Once you've built a few events, you'll want a quick way to see what's coming up, find past events, and make edits. Your Events list is home base for all of it.

## Finding Your List

Click **Events** in the left sidebar. You'll see every event you've created, newest first, with the title, date, and status (Published or Draft) at a glance.

## Editing an Event

1. Click any event title to open it
2. Make your changes (date, location, description, whatever)
3. Click **Update**

Changes go live immediately.

:::caution
Edits to recurring events apply to the whole series. Need to change just one date? Create a separate event for that occurrence instead.
:::

## Deleting an Event

Open the event and move it to the **Trash**. It's recoverable until you empty the trash, so no panic if you click the wrong one.

You don't actually need to delete past events — they stop showing on your events page automatically once the date passes.

## Filtering & Search

Use the **search bar** to find events by name, **sort by date** to scan upcoming or past events, and **filter by status** to see just Published or Drafts.
