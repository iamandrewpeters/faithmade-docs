# Event Registration

Your event looks great. Now you need people to actually sign up. Faithmade keeps registration simple: add a cost, customize your button, and link to wherever you handle signups.

## Adding a Cost

In the **Event Settings** panel, find the **Cost** field and type your price ("$25" or "$10 per person"). This displays on the event page so people know what to expect.

:::tip
Free event? Leave the Cost field blank. Faithmade won't show a cost line at all — no need to type "$0."
:::

## Customizing Your Button

The default button says "Register." Want something catchier? Use the **Button Text** field. "Reserve Your Spot" works great.

## Setting Your Registration Link

Paste your external registration URL into the **Registration Link** field. This can point to Planning Center, Eventbrite, a Google Form, or whatever your church uses.

Visitors click your button and land right where they need to sign up.

:::note
No registration link = no button on your event page. Make sure you include the full URL with `https://`.
:::
