# Choosing & Understanding Your Starter Site

Starter sites are pre-built church website templates that give you a gorgeous head start. No blank-page panic required.

## How It Works

Head to **Design > Starter Sites** to browse the gallery. Each one is a fully designed church website you can preview before committing.

Once imported, your site gets populated with demo content, sample pages, and placeholder images. Think of it like moving into a furnished apartment — everything's in place, you just swap in your own stuff.

## What to Do After Importing

1. Replace demo content with your real church info
2. Update your church name, address, and service times in **Church Info**
3. Swap placeholder images for your own photos
4. Customize colors and fonts to match your brand in the **Customizer**

## Good to Know

You can switch starter sites later, but importing a new one replaces your current content.

:::caution
If you've already done a bunch of customizing, screenshot your favorite parts before importing a new starter site.
:::
