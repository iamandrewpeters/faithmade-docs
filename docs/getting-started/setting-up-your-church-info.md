# Setting Up Your Church Info

Your church info is the foundation of your entire site. Set it once and it shows up everywhere automatically.

## Where to Find It

From your dashboard, look for the **Church Info** section in the sidebar.

## What to Fill In

1. **Church name** as you want it displayed
2. **Address** for maps and footer info
3. **Phone number**
4. **Email** for general inquiries
5. **Service times** that show up across your site
6. **Social media links** for Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, etc.

Once saved, this info auto-populates your header, footer, contact page, and anywhere else it's referenced. Change it in one place, it updates everywhere.

## Tips

- **Be specific with service times.** "Sundays at 9am and 11am" beats "Sunday mornings."
- **Use a monitored email.** If nobody checks it, don't list it.
- **Keep social links current.** If you haven't posted on Twitter since 2019, maybe skip that one.
