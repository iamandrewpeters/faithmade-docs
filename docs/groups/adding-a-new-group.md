# Adding a New Group

Small groups are the heartbeat of your church. Life groups, Bible studies, "that thing on Wednesday nights" — whatever your people call them, your site should make it easy to find one and jump in.

## How to Add a Group

1. Click **Groups** in your dashboard sidebar, then **Add New**
2. Give your group a clear, inviting title ("Tuesday Night Bible Study" beats "Group 7")
3. Use the block editor to write a description — paint a picture of what the group is about, who it's for, and why someone should show up
4. Upload a **Group Image** on the right side (real photos of real people always win)

## Group Details

Scroll down to fill in:

- **Regularity** — how often it meets
- **Day and Meeting Time**
- **Group Type** — Bible Study, Men, Women, Marriage, Parenting, or Sermon Based
- **Neighborhood**
- **Kid Friendly** toggle

:::tip
Landscape photos look best in the group card layout.
:::

Hit **Publish** when ready, or save as **Draft** to finish later.
