# Group Guides

Group Guides are resources built for small group leaders — discussion questions, study materials, leader notes, and reading plans.

## What Are Group Guides?

A Group Guide is a resource tied to a small group's curriculum or study. Common examples:

- Weekly discussion questions for a sermon series
- Chapter-by-chapter study guides
- Leader prep notes with icebreakers and prayer prompts
- Downloadable worksheets or reading plans

## Creating a Group Guide

1. Click **Resources** in the left sidebar
2. Click **Add New**
3. Give your guide a clear title (e.g., "James Study, Week 3: Discussion Questions")
4. Use the block editor to add content
5. Upload a Featured Image
6. Hit **Publish**

:::tip
Use a consistent naming format for multi-week studies — it makes them much easier to find and organize.
:::

## Organizing Your Guides

Use **Categories** (e.g., "Small Group Guides," "Life Group Materials," "Fall 2024 Study"). Set a category in the Resource editor's right sidebar.

## Tips for Great Group Guides

- Keep them scannable
- Include topic, passage, discussion questions, and a prayer prompt at minimum
- Update regularly as series change
- Add downloadable PDF versions when possible
