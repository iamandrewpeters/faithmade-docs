# Group Leader Information

Every group needs a point person. In Faithmade, that's your Group Leader.

## Adding a Group Leader

1. Open the group you want to edit
2. Scroll down to the **Group Leader** section
3. Start typing a name and select the leader from the list

You can add multiple leaders.

:::note
Group leaders need to have an account in your Faithmade system to appear in the list.
:::

## Setting the Primary Contact Email

Find the **Primary Contact Email** field in the Group Leader section and enter the email address visitors can use to reach out. Use whichever email the leader actually checks.

## How Leader Info Appears on Your Site

Visitors who click into a group will see the leader name(s) and contact email. Having a real person's name makes a group feel approachable and worth showing up to.

**Multiple leaders are fully supported.** Leader changes update immediately.
