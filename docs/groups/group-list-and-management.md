# Group List & Management

The Groups list in your dashboard is your command center for everything small groups.

## Your Groups List

Click **Groups** in the sidebar and you'll see every group you've created. Each one shows the title, status (Published or Draft), and date.

## Editing a Group

Click the group title to open it, make your changes, hit **Update** to save. Changes go live immediately.

## Deleting a Group

Move it to **Trash**. Groups hit the trash first, so you've got a safety net.

:::tip
If a group is just taking a summer break, switch it to **Draft** instead of deleting it. That way the content is ready to go when the group kicks back up.
:::

## Filtering and Search

Use the search bar to find groups by name, or filter by status to see only Published or Draft groups.

Quarterly check-in idea: Review your groups list once a quarter to keep meeting times, leaders, and descriptions accurate.
