# Group Meeting Location

When someone's browsing your groups, one of the first things they want to know is "where do I show up?" Your location settings make that crystal clear.

## Two Location Types

When editing a group, scroll to **Meeting Location** and pick one:

**In Person** — Fill in Meeting Place Name, Street Address, City, State, and Zip. This info shows right on your group listing.

**Online Only** — For virtual groups on Zoom, Google Meet, etc. Address fields disappear.

:::tip
Instead of putting your Zoom link in the public description, try "Meeting link provided after you sign up." This keeps your link from getting shared publicly.
:::

In-person groups show the place name and address. Online groups get an online label. Combined with the Neighborhood filter, visitors can find a group close to home.
