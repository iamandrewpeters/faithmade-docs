# Connecting Google Analytics

Google Analytics lets you see who's visiting your church website, what pages they're looking at, and how they found you.

## What You'll Need

A **GA4 Measurement ID** — it looks like `G-XXXXXXXXXX`. Create a free account at [analytics.google.com](https://analytics.google.com) if you don't have one.

## How to Connect It

1. Go to **Settings** in your Faithmade dashboard
2. Click on **Integrations**
3. Find **Google Analytics** in the list
4. Paste your GA4 Measurement ID
5. Make sure the toggle is switched on
6. Click **Save**

:::note
It can take up to 24 hours for data to start showing up in your Google Analytics dashboard after connecting.
:::

## What You Can Track

- Number of visitors per week
- Which pages get the most views
- Where visitors are coming from (search, social, direct)
- How long people stay on your site
