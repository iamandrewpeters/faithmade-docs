# Connecting Planning Center

If your church runs on Planning Center, this integration is going to make your life significantly easier. Connect once, and your groups, events, forms, and sermons sync right over.

## How to Connect

1. Go to **Integrations > Planning Center > Connect**
2. Sign in with your Planning Center account (you'll need admin or owner permissions)
3. Click **Authorize** to give Faithmade access
4. Choose which data types to sync (Groups, Events, Sermons)
5. Hit **Sync Now**

## How the Sync Works

Planning Center is the source of truth. It's a **one-way sync** — changes you make in PCO flow to your Faithmade site, not the other way around. Update an event in Planning Center and it updates on your website automatically.

Use the **Sync Now** button anytime you want to pull fresh data immediately.

:::tip
Keep your PCO data clean and your website stays clean automatically. It's the "make your bed once" approach to church websites.
:::
