# YouTube Integration

YouTube is Faithmade's richest integration. Connect your church's YouTube channel to import sermon videos, link livestream events, and keep everything in sync.

## What You Can Do

- **Sermon Import** — Pull YouTube videos directly into your sermon library with title, description, and thumbnail
- **Livestream Events** — Link a YouTube livestream to any event on your site
- **Sync Status** — See connection health at a glance

## How to Connect

1. Go to **Integrations** in your Faithmade dashboard
2. Find **YouTube** and click **Connect**
3. Sign in with the Google account that manages your church's YouTube channel
4. Authorize Faithmade to access your YouTube data
5. You'll be redirected back with a "Connected" status

:::note
Sign in with the Google account that owns or manages the church's YouTube channel. If it's a Brand Account, you may need to select the right channel during sign-in.
:::

## Importing Sermons from YouTube

1. Go to **Sermons > Add New Sermon**
2. Click **Import from YouTube**
3. Browse recent uploads or search by title
4. Select the video — Faithmade pulls in title, description, and thumbnail
5. Add series, speaker, date, and other details
6. Click **Save**

:::tip
You can always edit the imported title and description — YouTube titles tend to be SEO-heavy and may need a cleanup.
:::

## Linking a Livestream to an Event

1. Create or edit an **Event** in Faithmade
2. Find the **Livestream** option
3. Select **YouTube** as the source
4. Choose the upcoming livestream or paste the stream URL
5. Save

## Sync Status

- **Connected** — all good
- **Needs Reauthorization** — click Reconnect to refresh
- **Error** — usually a quick disconnect/reconnect fixes it

Disconnecting from YouTube won't remove previously imported sermons from your site.
