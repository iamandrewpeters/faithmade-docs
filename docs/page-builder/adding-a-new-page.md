# Adding a New Page

Need a new page on your site? You're about two minutes away from having one.

## How to Create a Page

1. Go to **Design > Pages**
2. Click **Add New**
3. Give your page a name (this becomes the URL too, so keep it clean)
4. Choose to start with a blank page or pick a template
5. Page Builder opens and you're off to the races

Add your content using modules (text, images, buttons, whatever you need), then hit **Done > Publish** when you're happy with it.

## One Important Thing

Creating a page doesn't automatically add it to your menu. Your new page exists, but visitors won't find it unless you add it to your navigation.

Head to **Design > Menus** to add your new page to the menu.

:::tip
Keep page names short and clear. "About" beats "About Our Church and What We Believe and Our History" every day of the week. Clean URLs and clean menus make everyone's life easier.
:::
