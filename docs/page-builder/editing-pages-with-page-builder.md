# Editing Pages with Page Builder

Page Builder is your visual drag-and-drop editor for designing pages. No code, no stress — just click and create.

## Getting Started

Go to **Design > Pages**, find the page you want to edit, and click to open it in Page Builder. You'll see a visual editor that shows your actual page as it'll look on your site.

## How It's Organized

Your page is built with **rows** and **columns**:

- **Rows** are horizontal sections that span the full width of the page
- **Columns** divide rows into side-by-side areas
- **Modules** are the content blocks you drop inside columns (text, images, buttons, videos, and more)

Think of it like stacking shelves: rows are the shelves, columns are the dividers, and modules are what you put on display.

## The Basics

1. **Add content** by clicking the + button to insert modules
2. **Edit anything** by clicking directly on it
3. **Move things** by dragging and dropping
4. **Save your work** by clicking Done, then Save Draft or Publish

## Tips for Pages That Look Great

- **Keep it simple.** White space is your friend. Resist the urge to fill every pixel.
- **Think mobile first.** Most visitors are on their phones. Preview before publishing.
- **Short paragraphs win.** Walls of text send people away fast.
