# Adding a New Sermon

Adding a sermon takes about two minutes. Your congregation gets a polished sermon page with video, audio, and notes automatically.

## How to Add a Sermon

1. From your Dashboard, click **Add Sermon**
2. Give it a title
3. Use the block editor to add sermon content or notes
4. Paste your YouTube or Vimeo link in the **Video** field
5. Upload or select your audio file if you have one
6. Set the **Date Preached** (required)
7. Pick your **Speaker** from the dropdown
8. Assign it to a **Series** if it belongs to one
9. Add a **Featured Image**
10. Fill in SEO fields if desired
11. Hit **Publish**

Your sermon automatically shows up on your Sermons page and inside whatever Series you assigned it to.

:::tip
Got your YouTube link handy? Paste it in first — it's the fastest way to get a sermon live.
:::
