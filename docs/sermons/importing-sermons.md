# Importing Sermons

Switching platforms? Moving over years of sermons sounds painful, but we made it painless. Import your whole sermon library in minutes with a CSV file.

## How to Import

1. Go to **Sermons > Import**
2. Upload your **CSV file**
3. **Map your fields** (match your CSV columns to Faithmade's sermon fields)
4. Review and confirm
5. Hit **Import**

Your sermons populate with titles, dates, speakers, series assignments, and media links all intact.

## CSV Tips

- One sermon per row
- Include columns for title, date preached, speaker, series, and video/audio URLs
- Dates should be in a standard format (MM/DD/YYYY works great)
- Double-check your video links before importing

:::tip
Export from your old platform first, then clean up the CSV in Google Sheets before importing. Five minutes of cleanup saves a lot of headaches.
:::
