# Managing Sermon Series

Sermon Series keep your messages organized and looking sharp. Visitors can browse by series, and everything groups together automatically.

## Creating a New Series

1. Go to **Sermons > Series** in your dashboard
2. Click **Add New Series**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name** (e.g., "Summer in the Psalms")
   - **Slug** (auto-generates from the name)
   - **Description** (a sentence or two)
   - **Date** (when the series kicked off)
   - **Series Image** (16:9 ratio looks best)
4. Save it

## How Sermons Get Grouped

When you add or edit a sermon, pick the Series from the dropdown. That sermon automatically appears on the Series page. No extra steps.

:::tip
Upload a solid 16:9 image for each series. It shows on the series listing page and makes your sermon archive look polished.
:::
