# Sermon List & Filtering

Got a growing sermon library? The Sermon List gives you a bird's-eye view of everything, with filters to find exactly what you need.

## Your Sermon List

Head to **Sermons** in your dashboard. You'll see a list with columns for Title, Series, Speaker, Plays, Channels, and Preached On.

## Filtering & Sorting

Use the filters at the top to narrow down by:
- **Date range**
- **Speaker**
- **Series**

Click any column header to sort; click again to reverse.

## Bulk Actions

Select multiple sermons using the checkboxes, then use bulk actions to make changes across several sermons at once.

:::tip
The **Preached On** column is your best friend for keeping things chronological. Sort by it to quickly find any sermon in your library.
:::
