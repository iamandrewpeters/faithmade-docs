# Sermon Notes

Sermon Notes give your congregation something to follow along with on Sunday and reference later. Think of them as the digital version of that bulletin insert, but way better looking.

## Adding Notes to a Sermon

1. Open any sermon from your **Sermons** list
2. Scroll to the **Sermon Notes** section
3. Use the editor to add your notes

You can format with **bold**, *italics*, lists, and headings. Keep it scannable — your people are going to read these on their phones during the message.

## How Notes Appear

Notes show up right on the sermon page, below the video or audio player. Clean, readable, and automatically styled to match your site.

:::tip
Write your notes like fill-in-the-blanks or bullet-point outlines. Congregations engage more when they have something to actively follow along with.
:::
