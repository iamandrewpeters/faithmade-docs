# Adding & Managing Resources

Resources let you share downloadable files right from your site. Sermon outlines, volunteer handbooks, study guides — if your people need it, you can share it.

## Creating a New Resource

1. Go to **Resources > Add New**
2. Give it a title your people will actually recognize
3. Add a short description
4. Upload your file (PDFs work great)
5. Add a featured image
6. Choose a category
7. Hit **Publish**

Your resources show up in a clean grid layout and visitors can filter by category.

## Managing Your Library

Edit any resource from the list view. Need to swap a file? Just upload the new version.

:::tip
Review your resources quarterly. Nothing says "we forgot about our website" like a 2019 VBS signup form still hanging around.
:::
