# Editing a Form

Every form on your Faithmade site is fully editable. Add fields, remove ones you don't need, change labels, and rearrange with drag-and-drop. No code required.

## Getting to the Form Editor

1. Click **Forms** in the left sidebar
2. Find the form you want to edit and click on it
3. You'll land in the form builder

## Adding a New Field

Drag a field type from the left panel and drop it where you'd like it in your form. Click the new field to customize the label, placeholder text, and settings.

## Available Field Types

Text Input, Text Area, Email, Phone, Dropdown, Checkboxes, Radio Buttons, Number, Date, File Upload.

## Editing a Field

Click any field to select it. The right panel shows settings for:
- **Label**
- **Placeholder**
- **Required** toggle
- **Help Text**

## Rearranging Fields

Drag and drop. The builder updates in real time.

## Removing a Field

Hover over the field, click the trash icon, and confirm.

## Saving Your Changes

Hit **Save** when done. Changes go live immediately.

:::tip
Visit your live site after editing to make sure everything looks right on mobile.
:::
